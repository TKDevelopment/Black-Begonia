The Experience

Wedding planning is full of moving pieces — exciting, beautiful, and sometimes a little overwhelming. We’re happy to meet you wherever you are in the process, whether you already have a fully-formed vision or just a feeling you want your florals to capture.

We build on your ideas, drawing inspiration from your venue, your aesthetic, the season, and the atmosphere you want your guests to step into. Every wedding is different, and all of our floral designs are custom — created to feel deeply personal, artful, and beautifully suited to your day.

Our hope is that throughout the process you feel supported, creatively inspired, and confident that your florals are in caring hands — and that when the day arrives, everything feels even better than you imagined.

The Packages

ENGAGEMENT FLORALS

Planning a proposal or surprise engagement celebration? Our engagement floral designs are created to help make the moment unforgettable. Whether you're planning an intimate proposal or celebrating the big “yes” with family and friends, flowers can transform the setting into something truly special.

Common designs include oversized hand-tied bouquets for the proposal moment, romantic floral installations for a scenic backdrop, or a small collection of arrangements for an engagement gathering with loved ones. Each design is customized to complement the location, style, and atmosphere you’re envisioning—whether that’s a waterfront proposal, a cozy dinner celebration, or a surprise gathering at home.

Engagement florals are typically available for studio pickup. Delivery and setup may be available depending on the date, location, and scope of the design.

BEGINNING AT $350

ELOPEMENT PACKAGE

Designed for intimate celebrations, courthouse weddings, and private vow exchanges, our elopement collection focuses on beautifully crafted personal flowers that create meaningful floral moments without the scale of a full wedding.

Typical inclusions may include a bridal bouquet, boutonniere, and a small ceremony floral accent such as a ground arrangement or altar piece/s. Additional personal flowers like bridesmaid bouquets, corsages, or extra boutonnieres can easily be added to suit your celebration.

This option is perfect for couples who want thoughtfully designed florals that feel refined and intentional while keeping the overall floral scope simple. Elopement packages are designed for studio pickup on the day of your event. Delivery may be available on select dates depending on our schedule and existing wedding bookings.

BEGINNING AT $650

CEREMONY FLORALS

Our ceremony floral package is designed for couples who want a beautifully styled ceremony space along with coordinated personal flowers for the wedding party. This option focuses on creating a romantic atmosphere for the moment you say “I do,” with floral design that enhances the setting and frames the ceremony beautifully.

Typically includes a bridal bouquet, bridesmaid bouquets, boutonnieres, corsages, and focal ceremony arrangements such as arbor florals, aisle arrangements, or statement ground pieces. These elements work together to create a cohesive and visually impactful ceremony backdrop.

Because the ceremony is often the most photographed and emotionally significant part of the day, we thoughtfully design florals that complement your venue, color palette, and overall aesthetic. This package is perfect for couples hosting a reception where florals are minimal, or for those planning to handle reception décor separately while still investing in a stunning ceremony design.

BEGINNING AT $2,800

FULL SERVICE WEDDINGS

For couples planning a fully designed floral experience, our full-service wedding package includes comprehensive floral styling across your ceremony and reception spaces.

This typically includes all personal flowers for the wedding party, ceremony installations, reception centerpieces, sweetheart or head table designs, bar and accent arrangements, and large-scale statement pieces tailored to your venue.

Every floral proposal is custom built around your guest count, venue, color palette, and design vision to ensure the entire celebration feels cohesive and elevated from ceremony through reception. To ensure the highest quality designs and full-service support, Black Begonia Floral Design requires a minimum investment for full wedding floral services.

Full-service weddings include delivery, onsite installation and floral styling, as well as end-of-evening breakdown and collection.
