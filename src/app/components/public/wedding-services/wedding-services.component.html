WEDDING SERVICES

The Experience

Wedding planning is all about the details - which might be right up your alley, or a whole lot of overwhelm. We’re happy to work at your speed, whether you have a fully-developed vision, or just a vague idea you’re wanting brought to life.

We build on your ideas, drawing inspiration from your venue, your goals for the aesthetic (Black tie? Boho? A forest fairyland?), and what florals and foliages are in season and best suited to the task, to deliver the floral package of your dreams. Just as every client and event is different, all of our designs and concepts are custom - we enjoy the creative process and making something just for you. Our hope is that you’ll feel like you have a partner in us, and that you’ll leave your event thinking your florals were exactly what you were thinking, only better!
