MEET BECCA

le Creative behind Black Begonia Florals

Hi! I'm Becca, the lead designer + owner of Black Begonia Florals, a boutique wedding and event floral design studio based in South County, RI.

If you're looking for romantic, whimsical wedding flowers and a Florist who is passionate about taking care of the Earth, you're in the right spot!

Here at Black Begonia I am committed to sustainability without compromising style and pride myself in creating lush florals filled with dimension and romance.

I source and provide you with the freshest local product and work hard with you to create a beautiful design that will work cohesively with your venue and vision.

I have worked under some of the top Floral Designers within the New England area to hone my craft, and started out on my own back in 2015.

When not flowering, I love to travel, cook and be outside. Keats said it well, "Give me books, French wine, fruit, fine weather and a little music played out of doors."

What I'm Passionate About...

Sustainability...

Here at Black Begonia Florals it is my goal to bring our couples romantic wedding flowers + designs while incorporating eco friendly solutions.

I've always been inspired by nature, and the very fact I get to use it to create art still overwhelms me. I care deeply about our planet and creating a business that gives back to nature.

I'm constantly striving to find ways to reduce my carbon footprint, whether it's in my personal life or in my business. I'm proud to say that all our arrangements are built foam free, that we compost all our green waste, and buy locally grown product whenever possible. We even started our own cutting garden!

Favorites

FAVORITE FLOWER?

My all time favorite blooms are daffodils, but to work with would have to be hellebores or cosmos.

FAVORITE MOMENT AT WEDDINGS?

I love a good reveal, when we let the couple see the flowers + setup before their guests.

FAVORITE PLACE TO TRAVEL?

I lived in Italy for 3 years, and Rome will always be my favorite!

FAVORITE ARRANGEMENTS TO MAKE?

Centerpieces and big floral installations are by far my favorite!

FAVORITE SEASON?

For flowers + weddings I'd say Spring, but in general I'm a huge fan of Fall.
