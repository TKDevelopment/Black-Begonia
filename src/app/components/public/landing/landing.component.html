ABOUT

MEET BECCA

le Creative behind Black Begonia Florals

Hi! I'm Becca, the lead designer + owner of Black Begonia Florals, a boutique wedding and event floral design studio based in South County, RI.

If you're looking for romantic, whimsical wedding flowers and a Florist who is passionate about taking care of the Earth, you're in the right spot!

Here at Black Begonia I am committed to sustainability without compromising style and pride myself in creating lush florals filled with dimension and romance.

I source and provide you with the freshest local product and work hard with you to create a beautiful design that will work cohesively with your venue and vision.

I have worked under some of the top Floral Designers within the New England area to hone my craft, and started out on my own back in 2015.

When not flowering, I love to travel, cook and be outside. Keats said it well, "Give me books, French wine, fruit, fine weather and a little music played out of doors."
