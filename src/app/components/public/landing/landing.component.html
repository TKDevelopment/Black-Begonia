MEET BECCA

le Creative behind Black Begonia Florals

Meet Becca, the lead designer & owner of Black Begonia Florals, a boutique Rhode Island wedding florist based out of Watch Hill. Serving couples throughout coastal New England, Becca primarily designs for venues located throughout Newport, Watch Hill, Bristol, Narragansett, and South Kingstown.

With more than 15 years of floral industry experience, Becca brings a thoughtful and refined approach to wedding floral design. She works closely and intentionally with each couple to create romantic, garden-style and coastal wedding flowers that reflect the unique atmosphere of their celebration.

Becca’s floral journey began at just fifteen years old with a high school floral design class. What started as a simple class quickly became a lifelong passion, designing her first wedding that same year. Finding success early inspired her to pursue floral design professionally and develop her craft ever since.

Over time, Becca worked in and managed several flower shops while designing weddings throughout Rhode Island and New England. Through these experiences, she built the foundation that eventually grew into Black Begonia, now a trusted name in custom wedding floral design.

Today, every couple works directly with Becca from the first consultation through delivery and installation. Because each event is personally designed and managed by her, only a limited number of weddings are taken on each season to ensure every couple receives attentive service and exceptional design.
